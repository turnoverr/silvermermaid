About Us

At Silver Mermaid, our flake is local and freshly cut. Our potato cakes are all handmade daily, our chips are gluten free, and we use cholesterol free oil to ensure you get the best fish and chips every single visit.

A family-owned Cranbourne institution for over 25 years — we take pride in quality ingredients and honest cooking. No shortcuts, just great fish and chips made the right way.
